About me

Hey there! I'm Mahlori, a passionate explorer at the intersection of technology and nature. Ever since I discovered the incredible possibilities computers offer, I've been hooked on leveraging their functionalities to solve problems and create solutions tailored to my needs. Whether it's developing custom programs or tinkering with existing tools, I thrive on the challenge of finding innovative solutions.

Beyond the digital realm, I find solace and inspiration in nature. Despite living in a place where owning a fish tank might seem out of the ordinary, I've always been drawn to the tranquility and beauty of aquatic life. My commitment to staying motivated and centered extends to my daily routine, which includes kickstarting my mornings with a combination of push-ups and meditation.

My journey in academia led me to the University of Johannesburg, where I earned a BSc in Physical Science. Building on this foundation, I further honed my skills at Umuzi Academy, specializing in Information Technology. Along the way, I've had the privilege of diverse professional experiences, from guiding students as a physics practical demonstrator at the University of Johannesburg to delving into data science at Telkom and contributing insights as a data worker at Mboweni Accountants.
